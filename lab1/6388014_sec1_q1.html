Myanmar, Yangon 1 Day Tour with Shwedagon Pagoda

Myanmar one-day trip What Mushroom Travel brings together is Shwedagon Pagoda, the sacred place of Burma.
The highlight of this place is Golden Phra Maha Chedi with a height of 105 meters, or equivalent to a 36-story building.
The top of the pagoda is decorated with sapphire gems and precious stones Moreover, the inside is also containing the Buddha's hair relics.
The octagonal base of the chedi is made of bricks covered with gold leaf Visitors can also go up to see the pagoda, but only men are allowed.
